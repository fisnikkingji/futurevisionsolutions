What We Do

Everything Your Restaurant Tech Stack Needs

From initial configuration to emergency troubleshooting, we cover the full spectrum of online ordering technology support.

Deep hands-on experience integrating with Toast, Aloha, Brink, Revel, and Olo - covering menu syncing, order injection, item modifiers, pricing overrides, and location configuration. We diagnose and resolve POS connectivity failures, order routing errors, and menu discrepancies that prevent restaurants from accepting online orders - often during peak dinner service. We also work directly with POS vendors' technical teams to resolve edge-case integration failures and undocumented API behaviors.

We resolve payment failures across Stripe, Heartland, and other third-party processors - preventing revenue loss and reducing customer-facing checkout errors. We investigate declined transaction patterns, tokenization issues, and processor timeout scenarios, coordinating with payment vendors to deploy fixes. We also support restaurants in configuring payment capture rules, tip handling, and refund workflows within the platform.

Full back-office configuration including menus, modifiers, delivery zones, hours, and payment settings. We handle technical onboarding for new clients - configuring everything within the back-office from day one - and we maintain it over time. We get your platform set up right the first time, and we keep it that way as your menu and operations evolve.

We troubleshoot API and webhook issues including failed order notifications, menu push failures, loyalty integration events, and third-party delivery platform callbacks (DoorDash, Uber Eats). We review API request and response logs to identify root causes of ordering failures, and coordinate with the engineering team when platform-side fixes are required - so nothing falls through the cracks.

We train your restaurant managers and staff on the platform - reducing recurring support requests and empowering your team to self-serve common issues with confidence. We also create internal knowledge base articles and troubleshooting runbooks tailored to your setup, so institutional knowledge stays with your team long after we've onboarded you.

Have a trusted resource on call who already understands your business. Whether it's a monthly advisory retainer or emergency support during a critical ordering outage - we're here. We served as the primary technical point of contact for high-volume restaurant accounts at Paytronix, providing rapid response during the moments that mattered most. That same responsiveness is what we bring to every client at Future Vision Solutions.
